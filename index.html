Who Am I?

My name is Gülnur Avcı, and I am studying electrical engineering at the Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science. I am a hardworking, innovative, resilient, detail-oriented, and imaginative individual always looking to learn new skills and share my ideas with others.

Being both a STEM-oriented and a creative person, I have found myself working on projects in all sorts of fields. For example, I discovered my love for programming through building video games throughout high school. I continue to pursue this interest in college through internships and other projects. On the other hand, I have learned to express my artistic side through digital media design - such as creating fun videos and graphics - and painting murals in my garage!

My newest endeavors include learning to make virtual reality games, 3D modeling for art and design purposes, coming up with engineering-related projects, mastering the Adobe Creative Suite software, and training to get my piloting license.

I made this page to share some of my favorite projects I have worked on over the years. In addition to seeing technical qualifications on paper, an image (or video) can speak a thousand words.
