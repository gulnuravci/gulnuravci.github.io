About

I have been painting with acyrlic paint on and off since freshmen year of high school. I recently transitioned to painting walls with my first painting of the 'Cherry Blossom Landscape' in my room wall. From there, I got the idea to turn our second garage into a cool hangout spot. The first, and main step, in this project was painting a different mural on every single wall. So far, I have painted 'Thinker Alien', 'Rick', and 'Tom and Jerry'. I am currently working on the fourth wall.
