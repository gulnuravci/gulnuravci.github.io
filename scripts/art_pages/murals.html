About

I've been working with acrylic paints sporadically since my freshman year of high school. In 2019, I made a shift to mural painting, starting with my first piece, the 'Cherry Blossom Landscape,' on my bedroom wall. This experience inspired me to transform my garage into a vibrant hangout space by adorning each wall with murals. These projects serve as my practice and preparation for creating larger and more remarkable murals throughout the city, aimed at enhancing the community's beauty and bringing joy to people."
