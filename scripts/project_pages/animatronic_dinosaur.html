About

Created the inner workings of an animatronic dinosaurs using Arduinos, servos, LEDs, and a motion sensor. When the motion detector senses movement by the viewer, a series of events are triggered: the meteor on the display starts spinning (can be viewed at the link above), the dinosaur slowly starts looking at the direction of the viewer (imitating fear), and finally the arms of the dinosaurs move hastly in a running motion. Unfortunately, I wasn't able to get a video of the final build of the project.
