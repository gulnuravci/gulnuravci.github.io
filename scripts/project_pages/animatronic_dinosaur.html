About

Created the inner workings of an animatronic dinosaur using Arduinos, servos, LEDs, and a motion sensor. When the motion detector senses movement by the viewer, a series of events are triggered: the meteor on the display starts spinning, the dinosaur slowly starts looking in the direction of the viewer (imitating fear), and finally, the dinosaur's arms move hastily in a running motion. Unfortunately, I wasn't able to get a video of the final build of the project.
